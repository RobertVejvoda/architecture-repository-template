# Business Policies

<!--
Purpose:
Record durable business rules that shape architecture decisions.
-->

| Policy | Rule | Owner | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Access Scope Policy | Users and operators may only access data and actions needed for their role and organizational scope. | Business Owner / Security Lead | Link to authorization and audit evidence. |
| Readiness Policy | Production or pilot traffic may start only after mandatory readiness checks pass or are formally waived. | Architecture Board | Waivers need owner, scope, expiry, and review date. |
| Evidence Policy | Architecture-significant controls require reviewable evidence before acceptance. | Architecture Owner | Link evidence from readiness, review, or delivery artifacts. |
